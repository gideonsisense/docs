Visualize Widget’s Queries

Note: The JAQLine add-on requires Sisense V6.6 or later.

The JAQLine add-on provides a visualization of the queries a widget performs towards an ElastiCube so you can understand which tables participate in the query, and which relationships between the columns of the tables are in-use by the widgets queries.

This plugin can help you troubleshoot your widget by displaying many-to-many relationships, and identify possible causes of slow query performance.

To install the JAQLine add-on:

  • Download the attachment and unzip the contents into your C:\Program Files\Sisense\PrismWeb\plugins\ folder. If the plugins folder doesn’t exist, just create it. After those files have been unzipped there, you may also have to restart the web server.
    Note: If you just created the plugins folder, you may have to restart the web server after unzipping the files.

To activate the JAQLine add-on, click on the menu of the widget, and then click Analyze Widget JAQL.

A pop up window will display the query visualization. Click outside the window to close it.
