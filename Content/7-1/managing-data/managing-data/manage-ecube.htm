From the Data page of , you can manage your s and view their current states.

The Data page contains a list of all the s you have created or that have been shared with you.

To locate s easily, on the right side of the page, you can view s you recently worked on, that were created by you, or that were shared with you. In addition, on the left side, you can locate an through the Search field.

Each tile contains information about the status of the , its owner, and who its shared with it. You can open your by clicking on its tile or you can manage your s by clicking and selecting one of the following options:

  • Stop: Click to stop the . s that have been stopped cannot be queried. This is useful for troubleshooting s and issues with your dashboard.
  • Restart: Click to restart an that you have stopped.
  • Dependencies: Click to see what entities are using the . Entities include data security rules, drill hierarchies, dashboards, widgets (from other dashboards), and Pulse alerts.
  • Schedule Build: Click to schedule builds to automatically synchronize with the underlying data source to ensure all data is up to date. See Scheduling Builds for more information.
  • Delete: Click to delete an . If the does not have any entities dependent (dependencies) on it, then you will be asked to confirm that you want to delete the . Otherwise, you will be prompted to remap your dependencies to another data source. See Deleting s for more information.
  • Cancel all Queries: Click to cancel all current queries to the .
  • Go to Admin: Opens the Data Sources section in the Admin page of the where you can manage your .
