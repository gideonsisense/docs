Changing a Widget’s Visualization

You can change the visualization of a widget (for example, from a Line Chart to a Pie Chart). When you change the type, all relevant values and definitions are transferred to the new visualization.

To change a widget’s visualization:

  1. Open the Widget Designer, as described in Opening the Widget Designer.
  2. Click the Visualization selector, shown below, to display a menu of visualization options.
  3. Select a different visualization, and click Apply.
