Dashboards

This section will show you how to get started working with your dashboards.

Creating Dashboards

Designers and Dashboard Designers can create dashboards from the Analytics page where all your existing dashboards are displayed.

When you create a dashboard, your dashboard is initially empty. The next step is to add widgets, or visualizations, to your dashboard to make it meaningful.
